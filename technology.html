    TECHNOLOGY

    Technology of tube includes extruding, heading, offset printing, silk screen printing, hot-stamping, labeling, holing/foil sealing/cap assembling, Sealing, Final inspection, which are the whole manufacture process of packaging tube. Offset printing is the most popular printing of cosmetic tubes, we are well equipped with eight colors offset printing and UV link lacquering machine. Silk screen printing make your products luxury because When you run your fingers over the art on the tube it is raised up and will always remain glossy, even on a matte finish tube.Hot stamping is usually used for logo printing, while words and image are also available. Labeling is applied for complicate design, especially for colorful images and vivid pattern.

      You can find more about tube technology below introductions and videos.

    1.Extruding
    We can make 2 layers and 5 layers PE tube. ABL tube, PBL tube, High gloss tube and aluminum tube can be maked. The tube color can be customized. CMYK and Pantone color are both acceptable.
    2. Heading
    We can make different heads according to the tube head design. And the head color can be customized, or you can adjust according the tube color. CMYK and Pantone are both acceptable.
    3.Offset printing
    We are well equipped with six color offset printing and lacquering machine where printing is done using UV inks and followed by UV lacquering to give the best properties. With good adhesion, Offset printing is suitable for multi - color printing and large block printing,
    4. Silk-screen printing
    When you run your fingers over the art on the tube it is raised up and will always remain glossy, even on a matte finish tube. We are well equipped with 6 color screen printing machine. With good stereo feeling,and strong covering power, Silk screen printing also can reflect vivid color on color tube and color difference is lesser.
    5. Holing/Foil sealing/Cap assembling
    We can make Dia.2-12mm hole and then foil the hole with aluminum sheet. The cap assembling can be done by machine too. That is we can hole, foil seal and cap assemble at the same machine.
    6. Hot-stamping
    Mostly apply for clients’ LOGO printing, We offers foil stamping as one of the decoration options, which adds glamour to your product. Foil stamping is the process in which golden, silver or color images are transferred from the foil to the tube through hot silicon rubber die. Foil stamping can be hot stamped on cap as well as tube. Small and simple area of foiling gives best results on tubes.
    7. Labeling
    Mostly used for complicated and colorful design. Offset printing, screen printing and foil stamping is also available on labels. Labeling as a decoration option gives you greater liberty while preparing the concept of artwork design. The effect of labeling have distance labeling and overlapping labeling.
    8. Sealing
    We have vertical sealing and special shape sealing. Vertical sealing is the most sealing type. Or you can create your own sealing shape. Any shape is available.
    9. Final inspection and packaging
    Final inspection is to pick out unqualified products. we use standard export carton and inner tray with polybag to pack the tube.
