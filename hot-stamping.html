    Customized color and pattern hot stamping machine

    We offers hot stamping as one of the decoration options, which adds glamour to your product. Hot stamping is the process in which golden, silver or other color images are transferred from the foil to the tube through hot silicon rubber plate. Hot stamping can be stamped on cap as well as tube. Small and simple area of hot stamping gives best results on tubes, we can also provide hot stamping for words which can make your products more special.

    Hot stamping is mostly used for LOGO printing which can make your logo more obvious and attractive. Then it can make your products perfect.
