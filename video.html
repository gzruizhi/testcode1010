    Automatically PE tube extruding machine
    We have both PE tube extruding machine and welded tube extruding machine. The diameter we make is from 13mm to 60mm, the capacity can be from 3ml to 400ml. PE tube extruding machine can make 2 layers and 5 layers tube. The color can be customized, Pearl color is also available. That is say, the tube color can be all pantone color or you can provide standard sample for color reference. What’s more, The tube thickness and hardness can also be customized. Welded tube extruding machine are used for ABL(aluminum barrier laminated) tube, PBL（Plastic Barrier Laminated) tube and high gloss tube etc. PE tube extruding machine and welded tube extruding machine works different. Because welded tube have joint line while PE tube doesn’t have.
    High efficiency heading machine for PE tube and Welded tube
    Heading machine is to make a head for the tube to match the cap which can make normal cap and special cap depending on the cap type. The head can be made customized colors, all the pantone colors are ok, or you can provide standard sample for color reference. Heading is the second procedure of manufacturing PE tube. We need to make sure the compatibility of cap and tube, then when heading, there are lots of things we need to test, such as the compatibility of cap and tube, color difference, leakage etc. Welcome to visit our factory all the time.
    Six colors offset printing machine
    When functional and aesthetic requirements come together, a printed tube becomes the best option. We offer printed tubes with various tube colors options and printing color options. We are well equipped with six color offset printing and lacquering machine where printing is done using UV inks and followed by UV lacquering to give the best properties. Our printing machines are equipped to print halftone printing and solid printing with equal consistency. Offset printing is the most popular and normal printing for cosmetic plastic tube, When you run your fingers over the art on the tube you can’t feel anything because the printing link just like deep into the tube. Most people will choose offset printing for their cosmetic tube.
    Germany imported silk-screen printing machine for welded tube
    Silk screen printing is born for high end packaging. When you run your fingers over the art on the tube it is raised up and will always remain glossy, even on a matte finish tube. We are well equipped with 6 colors silk screen printing machine where printing is done using UV screen inks and followed by UV & Matt lacquering to give the best properties. Silk screen printing can make your products more high end, luxury and elegant because you can feel the printing and the color is more vivid than offset printing.
    Customized color and pattern hot stamping machine
    We offers hot stamping as one of the decoration options, which adds glamour to your product. Hot stamping is the process in which golden, silver or other color images are transferred from the foil to the tube through hot silicon rubber plate. Hot stamping can be stamped on cap as well as tube. Small and simple area of hot stamping gives best results on tubes, we can also provide hot stamping for words which can make your products more special. Hot stamping is mostly used for LOGO printing which can make your logo more obvious and attractive. Then it can make your products perfect.
    Left direction labeling machine with whole and partial effect
    When artwork becomes the answer for differentiation and shelf visibility, label tubes become the ideal option. These label tubes are available from 19mm to 50mm diameter. We also offer options like Opaque, Translucent and Transparent with Matt, Gloss or Metallic print. Offset printing, screen printing and hot stamping is also available on labels. Labeling as a decoration option gives you greater liberty while preparing the concept of artwork design. Labeling is applied for complicate design, especially for colorful images and vivid pattern.
