    by：Jimmy.Woo 594 browse Time required for reading：30s

    Dear Customers,


    Ruizhi Packaging Team will attend a beauty show--- Cosmoprof Bologna Italy on March 16th~18th.


    Site: Hall 20 Booth C9.


    It is our honor to meet you during the Cosmoprof Bologna. We will show our eco-friendly packaging to all visitors and present our new, unique, and innovative packaging.

    COSMOPROF WORLDWIDE BOLOGNA AT A GLANCE

    As the most influential global event dedicated to all facets of the beauty industry, Cosmoprof Worldwide Bologna has been a landmark event for more than 50 years.

    Cosmoprof is where companies do business and the perfect stage for beauty trendsetters to present breakthrough product launches and innovative solutions.

    Don't miss it!
